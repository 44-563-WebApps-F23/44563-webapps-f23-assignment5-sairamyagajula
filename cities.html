Row with cities breaks first

As the size of the browser window gradually reduces, it reaches a point where there isn't enough horizontal space to accommodate the full-width "col-md" divs for the city names. When this happens, the city name divs start to stack vertically, causing the row to break. On the other hand, the "col-sm-2" divs for the letters are designed to be more flexible and can continue to fit within the available space on smaller screens, so they break later.
